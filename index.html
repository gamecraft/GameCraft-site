Who are we?

We're a Bulgarian startup with a huge background
experience in the fields of game theory and design
and agile programming. Our team is focused on the
business model - gamification as a service while we
have a strong partnership with Camplight

What we do?

We are offering Gamification as a Service.
We create games from real-life activities - like
events, trainings, marketing campaigns, workflows
by making them more engaging and fun.

What is Gamification?

Gamification involves applying game design
thinking to non-game applications to make them
more fun and engaging.
Gamification refers to incorporating game elements
and mechanics into non-gaming websites and
software.
