About us

We are offering Gamification as a Service. We create games for real-life activities - like events, trainings and marketing campaigns by making them more engageing, visually appealing and fun. We create game layers for web-sites by using Gamification and Game-Design techniques. Our target customers are all businesses that want to make their product or service better.
